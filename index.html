About Denise

At the age of 11, I learned to program in HTML. Ever since then, I've had a voracious interest in software development.

In 2012, at age 14, I began my studies as a dual enrollment student at Florida Atlantic University. If you have any questions about dual enrollment, please email me at dkutnick2012@fau.edu. I plan to graduate from FAU in December 2015 with my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.

I work on at least one web project per year and group projects are my favorite. A backwards chronological list of sites that I made and/or contributed to is under my portfolio, and the most impressive websites are also listed in my resume.

I have several hobbies outside of computer science. Are you interested?
